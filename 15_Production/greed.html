Seven Sins 2017 - Greed - Watercolor

In the fall of 2016 I was tasked by my professor with doing a modern interpretation of the “Seven Deadly Sins”. The objective behind the assignment was to practice critical and creative thinking while working within a short window of time. I completed the seven images within a two week timeframe. They are a mix of pen and ink and watercolor. The height of the 2016 presidential election gave me all of the inspiration I needed to complete these honest political caricatures.
