Sketches 2017 - Mixed Media

It is my goal to fill an entire sketchbook with portraits before the end of my senior year at Massart. I have never been able to fill an entire sketchbook cover to cover and I refuse to purchase another until I complete this one. I want to have a better understanding of facial anatomy and the way it is affected by different lighting scenarios. Most of the portraits are begun under direct observation and completed with the aid of photographs.
