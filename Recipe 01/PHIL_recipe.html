Classic Beef Stroganoff Recipe

Recipe posted by food_lover123

Quick cooking tips!

Prep Time: 20 min

Total Time: 38 min

Serves: 6 people

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds beef sirloin steak, 1/2 inch thick
  • 8 oz fresh mushrooms, sliced (2 1/2 cups)
  • 2 medium onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 1/2 cups Progressoâ„¢ beef flavored broth (from 32-ounce carton)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 3 cups of egg noodles

Steps

  1. Cut beef across grain into about 1 1/2 x 1/2-inch strips.

  2. Cook mushrooms, onions and garlic in butter in 10-inch skillet over medium heat,
    stirring occasionally, until onions are tender; remove from skillet.

  3. Cook beef in same skillet until brown. Stir in 1 cup of the broth, the salt and Worcestershire sauce.

  4. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer 15 minutes.

  5. Boil egg noodles

  6. Stir remaining 1/2 cup broth into flour; stir into beef mixture. Add onion mixture; heat to boiling, stirring constantly.

  7. Boil and stir 1 minute. Stir in sour cream; heat until hot (do not boil). Serve over noodles.
